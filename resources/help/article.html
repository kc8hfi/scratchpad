The ARTICLE part:




  • This is the area that you can type in the information that pertains to the selected node.  
  • It is a basic text editor.

  1. Right Click in the Article Area
    • Menu, with cut, copy, paste
  2. Tool bar
    • Menu options for cut, copy, paste
copy - copies the selected text to the clipboard.
cut - cuts the selected text f rom the article, puts the text in the clipboard.
paste - pastes the contents of the clipboard at the cursor position.


Saving the Article Text

There are 4 ways to save the information in the article area. The article is associated with the selected treenode.  
  1. Click the icon on the toolbar
  2. Right-click in the article area, then click the menu item to save
  3. from the tools menu, choosing the "save article" option
  4. Click a different treenode
Icon on the Toolbar


Click this button to save the article to the selected node.  



Right-click in the article area

Right-click in the article area, click the "Save Article" menu option to save the article.



Click on the Tools menu


Choose the "Save Article" menu item from the tools menu.  This will save the article



Click on a different treenode
1.  Changed the article that is associated with a node
                   

2.  Click on a different treenode to save the Article area for the previous node.
